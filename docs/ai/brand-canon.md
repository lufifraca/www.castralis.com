## Part 1 — Strategic Foundations & Executive Narrative

### 1.1 The Narrative Thesis

In the Roman Empire, the frontier wasn't secured by a single, monolithic capital asset, but
by the **castra** — a network of highly organized, self-sustaining fortresses linked by the
**limes**, the strategic transit corridors and tactical roadways of the frontier.

Castralis is the modern industrial equivalent. We engineer **power-aware, grid-edge
distributed compute infrastructure** designed for the physical realities of the modern
enterprise and energy grid. By utilizing existing civil and industrial infrastructure,
Castralis deploys modular, mutually reinforcing compute nodes that turn localized data
footprints into **sovereign domains of resilience**. We do not depend on the core; we defend
and process at the perimeter.

### 1.2 The Core Value Proposition

Centralized cloud architecture has hit an unyielding physical wall. The geometric expansion
of high-density AI workloads has accelerated a direct collision with the physical limits of
civil infrastructure: **power-grid congestion, thermal saturation, real-estate scarcity, and
speed-of-light backhaul latency.**

Castralis bypasses the centralized crisis entirely by enforcing **Absolute Domain Control**.
We deliver modular, power-aware distributed compute infrastructure that transforms passive
industrial real estate, stranded renewable generation, and underutilized utility capacity
into high-yield, sovereign compute assets.

We completely reject consumer-tech hyperbole, software-as-a-service clichés, and speculative
Web3/crypto aesthetics. Castralis operates with the **quiet confidence, permanence, and
deterministic language of heavy utility asset management.**

### 1.3 Target Audience Profile & Market Positioning

| Audience | Who | What they care about |
|---|---|---|
| **Enterprise Buyers** | CIOs, CDOs, CTOs | Operational uptime, eliminating backhaul latency, slashing data transit/bandwidth costs, strict regulatory compliance through geographical sovereignty |
| **Infrastructure Capital** | Institutional investors, energy producers, REITs | Asset utilization, capex fragmentation, mitigating macroeconomic/grid interconnection queues, capturing immediate yield from stranded power or passive rights-of-way |

## Part 2 — Core Brand Attributes

1. **Territoriality & Domain Control** — Absolute visual and operational sovereignty over
   data and compute at the exact point of ingestion.
2. **Resilience & Redundancy** — Fail-safe by architectural design. The loss of a single
   node does not degrade the integrity of the network; the mesh heals and re-routes
   seamlessly.
3. **Power-Aware & Grid-Edge Native** — Dynamically matches massive computational loads with
   real-time localized energy availability, thermal thresholds, and grid constraints.
4. **Independence** — Complete self-sustenance. Nodes are engineered to execute
   mission-critical workflows independently during total backhaul disconnection.
5. **Modularity** — Plug-and-play, standard physical dimensions built to lock directly into
   existing civil infrastructure footprints.

## Part 3 — Verbal Identity & Messaging Architecture

### 3.1 Register & Tone

**Tone:** engineered, institutional, infrastructure-grade, solid, quietly confident.

**DO:**
- Speak with the weight of an infrastructure asset manager.
- Use precise, deterministic, colder engineering language.
- Focus on capacity metrics, thermodynamic survival, asset lifecycles, and localized
  load-shedding.

**DON'T (banned):**
- Consumer-tech / SaaS hype words: *revolutionary, seamless, magical, disruptive.*
- Treating AI as a nebulous cloud concept — it is a **heavy physical workload** with intense
  thermal, spatial, and electrical consequences.
- Soft greenwashing: *sustainability, eco-friendly optimization* → replace with
  **"Grid-Arbitrage Resilience"** and **"Thermodynamic Survival."**

## Part 4 — Visual Identity System (Canonical Design Tokens)

### 4.1 Color Palette

Dark-mode anchored — subterranean concrete, command centers, industrial steel.

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Deep Navy** | `#13294B` | Primary structural fields, high-density oxide materials, institutional depth |
| **Slate** | `#26303F` | Secondary hardware enclosures, anodized aluminum shells, mechanical components |
| **Ice Blue** | `#C9D7EC` | Supporting data streams, schematic lines, drafting notations, thermal cooling indicators |
| **Amber/Bronze** | `#B5660A` | **The exclusive energy accent.** Reserved entirely for power-aware activation points, localized data cores, and system status nodes |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Technical text readability, technical line work, contrast in documentation |

Rule: Amber/Bronze is *scarce by definition* — it marks live power/compute, nothing else.

### 4.2 Typography Hierarchy

| Layer | Canonical face | Treatment |
|---|---|---|
| Wordmark & display headers | **Söhne** (Breit / Halbfett) or **Inter** (Bold) | Always uppercase, wide deliberate tracking — unyielding architectural permanence |
| Body narrative copy | Brutalist, authoritative contemporary **serif** — **Bradford** or **Feijoa** | Institutional weight, balancing the utilitarian sans layouts |
| Data / code metrics | **Roboto Mono** or **SF Mono** | Technical specs, grid references, electrical loads |

**Anti-persona (strictly prohibited):** rounded "crypto" fonts, ultra-futuristic tech fonts
with clipped/missing crossbars, heavy gradients, 3D shadows, neon glows.

*Implementation note (not canon, practical):* Söhne, Bradford, and Feijoa are commercial
(Klim / Lineto) — require licensed webfonts. Free-tier fallback stack: **Inter** (display)
+ a licensed serif decision + **Roboto Mono** (data). Flag for founder decision — see §7-C.

### 4.3 The Core Emblem — "The Kinetic Conduit"

The trademark-defensible brand mark is a geometric evolution of a Roman fort groundplan
(*castrum*):

- A **square outline with softly rounded corners** (the classic "playing-card" shape).
- The internal horizontal and vertical roads (*cardo* and *decumanus*) are engineered into a
  **concentric maze layout**: vector lines with **sharp, right-angle offsets** that step
  around one another in a precise interlocking flow.
- All lines converge on a single, proud, **solid Amber/Bronze diamond** at the center — an
  active, grid-edge computational hub.
- This construction deliberately **solves and replaces the generic target/reticle or
  cross-window cliché.** No diagonals, no X.

## Part 5 — Product Nomenclature & System Requisites

### 5.1 The Castralis Praesidium

- **Classification:** Hardened Grid-Edge Compute Node Enclosure.
- **Physical realization:** Weather-hardened, anti-tamper **steel and anodized aluminum**
  chassis on a strict **2:2:1 structural bounding ratio (220mm × 220mm × 110mm)**. Vertical
  **heat-sink flutes on its flanks**; a **top-ventilation deck machined in the exact image
  of the Kinetic Conduit emblem**.
- **Operational specs:** High-density **10kW → 100kW+** localized AI inference workloads.
  Deterministic operation between extreme thresholds (**−40°C to +55°C**) via integrated
  **zero-water closed-loop ambient cooling** architecture.

### 5.2 The Castralis Limes

- **Classification:** Autonomous Mesh Routing & Energy Arbitrage Protocol.
- **Operational specs:** The **localized software fabric** that coordinates independent
  Praesidium units. Executes **real-time grid arbitrage** by dynamically throttling or
  migrating compute tasks based on utility volatility and spot-pricing, while guaranteeing
  **fault-tolerant self-healing routing** if an individual node is severed from the mesh.

### 5.3 The Castralis Principia

- **Classification:** Institutional Enterprise Command Interface.
- **Operational specs:** The centralized enterprise visualization portal. Real-time
  infrastructure telemetry, localized **Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE)** tracking,
  **carbon-intensity statistics**, and **cryptographic domain validation maps**.

## Part 7 — Deviation Register: live site vs. canon

Compiled read-only against the current site. Each item is work for the "implement
correctness" pass. ⚑ = founder decision needed before implementing.

### 7-0. Founder decisions — 2026-07-04 (all ⚑ resolved)

1. **Fonts → free fallback stack.** No paid licenses. **Inter** (display, uppercase, wide
   tracking) · a free **serif** for body narrative (candidate: *Newsreader* or *Fraunces* —
   confirm at build) · **Roboto Mono** (data/metrics). Resolves 7-C.
2. **Status colors → Amber & White only.** Strictly on-palette; **no red/green anywhere.**
   Form errors render in Amber/Bronze `#B5660A`, success in White/Ice Blue. `--ok`/`--alert`
   green/red are removed. Resolves the ⚑ in 7-B.
3. **Glow aesthetic → KEEP, as a documented exception.** The luminous HUD look (gold glows,
   soft blurs, gradient shine, scan sweeps) is *retained by explicit founder choice*, even
   though §4.2 bans glows/gradients/3D-shadows. **This is the one standing exception to the
   canon** — §4.2's anti-persona rules still govern the *logo* and any print/deck output, but
   the website keeps its glow layer. Resolves the ⚑ in 7-D (outcome: no change to effects).
4. **Boot/decrypt intro → SHORTEN + de-spy** (revised 2026-07-04; was "remove entirely").
   Kept but trimmed to 4 fast lines and reframed from espionage ("secure channel / decrypt /
   clearance granted") to an engineering power-on ("power-on self-test / thermal loop nominal /
   mesh sync locked"). Resolves the ⚑ in 7-E (dossier conceit).
   ⚠ Because the overlay is retained and JS-gated, the audit-#1 black-screen risk is NOT

Everything else in §7 (A logo, B palette values + amber-exclusivity, C/E copy, F geometry,

### 7-C. Typography — HIGH ⚑
- Live: Space Grotesk (display) + JetBrains Mono (everything else). Canon: Söhne/Inter Bold
  display · Bradford/Feijoa **serif body** · Roboto Mono/SF Mono data.
- Live hero/headers use **negative tracking** (`styles.css:167` `-.02em`); canon demands
  **wide deliberate tracking** on uppercase display.
- Body narrative set in mono violates the serif-body hierarchy; mono is for metrics only.
- ⚑ Söhne/Bradford/Feijoa are paid faces: license them, or lock the fallback stack
  (Inter + serif TBD + Roboto Mono)?

### 7-D. Anti-persona effects — MEDIUM ⚑
Canon bans **neon glows, heavy gradients, 3D shadows** (§4.2). Live site leans on all
three: gold glow shadows (`--gold-glow` used ~12×), additive-blend glow sprites
(`main.js:728`), cyan holo-scan sweeps (`styles.css:350–353`), gradient gem/fills
(`logo.svg`, boot bar, meters). ⚑ Ruling needed: reinterpret the HUD style as restrained
drafting-table line work (Ice Blue lines, flat Amber points), or accept a documented
exception for the site's "live telemetry" conceit.
